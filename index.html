Pip & Clef — there is a world hidden between the notes.

In the spaces music leaves behind lies the Resonance — a living world held together by one Great Score. Its pages are lost, and two friends must bring them home. Illustrated adventure stories for ages 5–11, ABRSM Prep–Grade 1.

Where every adventure begins

Deep in a hollow hill, Professor Crotchet sits in his study with the two friends listening closely. He explains that something in the Resonance has gone wrong. “That,” he says, “you will have to discover for yourselves.” Then he strikes the tuning fork.

Book one · The Clockwork Moors — pulse and beat

Every mill and machine of the Moors once moved to one shared beat — until the great Pendulum fell still, and nothing would fit together again.

Book two · The Stave Islands — the stave and note names

Five islands rest in a bright sea, each with its own name and voice — but the Treble Clef Lighthouse has gone dark, and no one recalls who lives where.

Book three · The Drum Caldera — note and rest values

In a drumming volcanic crater the Calderans speak in sounds long and short — and in the held breaths between them. Then the longest and shortest sounds were stolen, and the meaning fell apart.

First Notes · Collection One

Three worlds. Three pages carried home. Collection One gathers the first three adventures — pulse, the stave, and the lengths of sound — where music theory is lived long before it is ever named.
